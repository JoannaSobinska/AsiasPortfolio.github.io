Snake Game

Project description

This classic arcade-inspired game brings the iconic Snake experience to life through Python programming. Users can maneuver the snake through a dynamic grid, collecting food items to grow longer while avoiding collisions.

Key features

  • Responsive Design: Enjoy a seamless gaming experience across various devices with a responsive design that adapts to different screen sizes.
  • Score Tracking: Challenge yourself and others by tracking high scores, adding a competitive edge to the gameplay.
  • Smooth Animations: Experience fluid animations that enhance the visual appeal of the game, from the snake's movements to the appearance of food items.
